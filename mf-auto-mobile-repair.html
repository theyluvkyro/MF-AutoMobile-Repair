What We Fix

Our Services

Most common repairs done right at your location. If it can be safely done outside a shop, Zoran handles it — saving you the tow and the inflated shop rates.

Brake Repair

Pads, rotors, calipers, and lines inspected and replaced on-site for your safety.

Alternator Replacement

Battery keeps dying? Dashboard lights flickering? We diagnose and replace your alternator on location.

Starter Replacement

Car won't crank? A bad starter is a common fix we handle right in your driveway.

Diagnostics

Check engine light on? We run a full scan and explain exactly what's going on — no guesswork.

Suspension Repair

Rough ride or pulling to one side? We inspect and repair shocks, struts, and suspension parts.

Tune-Ups

Spark plugs, filters, and fluid checks to keep your engine running clean and efficient.

Battery Issues

Dead battery or corroded connections? We test, charge, and replace batteries on the spot.

Roadside Repair

Broken down somewhere? Call us. We come to your location and get you moving again.
